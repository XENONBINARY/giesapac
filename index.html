What is Gies APAC?

Gies APAC is a student-led regional chapter supporting Gies graduate students across the Asia-Pacific region through networking opportunities, shared resources, and community events. Open to all current students and alumni in the iMBA, iMSA, and iMSM programs. Gies APAC is also a proud member of the iGBA International Chapter Leadership committee, which coordinates Gies iDegrees engagement and activities for members located outside of North America.
