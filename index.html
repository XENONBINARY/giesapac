What is Gies APAC?

Gies APAC is a student-led regional chapter supporting Gies graduate students across the Asia-Pacific region through networking opportunities, shared resources, and community events. Open to all current students and alumni in the iMBA, iMSA, and iMSM programs. Gies APAC also operates under the iGBA International Chapter Leadership Committee, which coordinates Gies iDegrees engagement and activities for members located outside of North America.
